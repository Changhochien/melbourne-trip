# Day 1 — Jul 1 (Tuesday): Arrival

> Flights: Scoot TR873 TPE→SIN 01:35→06:00 | TR38 SIN→MEL 11:05→20:20  
> Arrival estimate: ~21:00 MEL after immigration + bags

## Tonight — Light and Easy

You're coming off a red-eye via Singapore. Don't plan anything ambitious tonight.

### Plan

| Time | Activity |
|------|----------|
| 20:20 | Arrive MEL T2 |
| 20:45–21:15 | Immigration, bag claim |
| 21:15–21:45 | Clear customs, find SkyBus |
| 21:45 | SkyBus to Southern Cross Station |
| ~22:15 | Arrive Southern Cross, walk to hotel |
| ~22:30 | Check in to CBD hotel |
| ~23:00 | Light dinner nearby or hotel restaurant |

## Getting From Airport

| Option | Price (TWD) | Notes |
|--------|------------|-------|
| **SkyBus** ✅ | ~NT$559 one way / ~NT$936 return | Every 10 min, runs to ~1 AM |
| Taxi / Rideshare | ~~NT$1,123–NT$1,572 | Not worth it for budget travellers |

**SkyBus** is the go. Runs every 10 minutes from T2 direct to Southern Cross Station (~30 min). Buy return to save — ~NT$936 return vs ~NT$1,118 two singles.

**Website:** skybus.com.au  
**Buy:** At the SkyBus counter in T2 arrivals, or online before you arrive.

## Accommodation — Melbourne CBD

Booked separately. See [[melbourne-accommodation-areas]] for CBD vs Fitzroy vs St Kilda comparison.

Target: **Space Hotel, Mantra on Little Collins, or YHA Metro** — ~~NT$3,818–NT$4,492/night in July. Central, good tram connections.

## Myki Card

Buy at MEL Airport on arrival (at 7-Eleven or Skybus counter):
- Load **~NT$673–NT$898** to start
- Zone 1 covers all Melbourne urban travel
- Free Tram Zone covers CBD (no Myki needed for trams within the CBD)

See: [[melbourne-transport-myki]]

## What to Do If You're Not Hungry

Sometimes after a long flight you're not hungry but just want to decompress. Hotel restaurant, a 7-Eleven sandwich, or just rest. That's fine. Day 2 is the big start.

## Luggage Storage

If you arrive early and can't check in yet, most CBD hotels store bags for free. Ask at check-in.

## What You'll See

- **Lorne** — Cute coastal town, good coffee stop, scenic pier
- **Apollo Bay** — Lunch spot on the beach, fish and chips recommended
- **Twelve Apostles** — The iconic limestone stacks, best in afternoon light
- **Loch Ard Gorge** — Dramatic canyon where the ship Loch Ard ran aground 1878. Rich history + stunning geology.

## Packing for Today

This is non-negotiable in July:
- **Rain jacket** — Not umbrella (wind makes it useless)
- **Warm layers** — It will be 10-15°C at best, wind chill makes it feel colder
- **Scarf / buff** — Coastal wind is relentless
- **Comfortable shoes** — Boardwalks can be wet
- **Sunscreen** — Even winter sun is strong near the coast
- **Camera** — Twelve Apostles at golden hour is spectacular

## What to Know

- Tour includes transport + guide. Lunch is extra.
- The road is winding — take motion sickness pills if you're sensitive
- Sunset is ~5:15 PM — the drive back will be dark. Bring a light snack.
- Mobile reception is patchy on the Great Ocean Road

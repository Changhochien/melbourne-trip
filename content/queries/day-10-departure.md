# Day 10 — Jul 10 (Thursday): Departure

> Flight: Scoot TR39 MEL→SIN 21:35 → TR874 SIN→TPE 09:30+1 (Jul 11)  
> SkyBus: ~30 min from Southern Cross to MEL T2  
> **Leave Southern Cross by 2:00–2:15 PM at latest.**

## Day 10 Plan

### Morning (8:00–11:00 AM)

| Time | Activity |
|------|----------|
| 8:00 AM | Breakfast at hotel |
| 8:30 AM | **Hosier Lane** — street art, arrives ~9 AM, no crowds |
| 9:30 AM | **Melbourne Central** — last-minute shopping, Emporium |
| 10:30 AM | Return to hotel, check out |
| 11:00 AM | Store luggage at hotel (most CBD hotels store free) |
| 11:30 AM | Optional: State Library reading room (15 min walk) |
| 12:30 PM | Lunch — Degraves Street laneway or Fed Square area |

### Getting to the Airport

| Option | Price (TWD) | Time |
|--------|-------------|------|
| **SkyBus** ✅ | ~NT$936 return × 2 = ~NT$1,873 (or ~NT$559 × 2 one-way) | ~30 min |
| Taxi / Rideshare | ~~NT$1,123–NT$1,572 | 30–45 min |
| Hotel shuttle | Varies | Varies |

**SkyBus from Southern Cross Station** — runs every 10 minutes, 4 AM to 1 AM.  
**Website:** skybus.com.au

### Departure Timing

| Time | Activity |
|------|----------|
| 12:30 PM | Finish lunch |
| 1:00 PM | Walk to Southern Cross Station |
| 1:30 PM | SkyBus from Southern Cross |
| 2:00 PM | Arrive MEL T2 |
| 3:00–4:00 PM | Check-in for Scoot flight (opens ~3 hours before) |
| 9:35 PM | Scoot TR39 departure |

**Note:** Check Scoot check-in opening time on their app closer to the date. Usually opens 3 hours before departure.

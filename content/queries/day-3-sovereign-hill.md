## What's There

- **Gold panning** — Try your luck, included in admission
- **Underground mine tour** — Guided descent into a 1850s mine. Excellent.
- **Period costume town** — Blacksmith, candle maker, sweets shop, costumed staff
- **Gold Museum** — Adjacent, included. Exhibits on gold rush history.
- **AURA** — Night sound and light show. Run separately (evening entry extra).
- **Wadawurrung Cultural Precinct** — Learn about the Traditional Owners of Ballarat region.

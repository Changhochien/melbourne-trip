# Phillip Island — Penguin Parade

## Overview

Phillip Island is 90 minutes south of Melbourne, connected by bridge. Home to the largest little penguin colony in the world. The **Penguin Parade** is the iconic nightly event where penguins waddle from the ocean to their burrows at sunset.

## Recommendation

**Penguins Plus** (+~NT$336 vs General) is worth it. Elevated platform means less crowd, better sightlines, more comfortable. Underground is nice-to-have, not essential. Guided tours are for those who want the full premium experience.

**July packing:** Full thermal layers, windproof jacket, beanie, gloves, scarf. 5–8°C with Southern Ocean wind = brutal.

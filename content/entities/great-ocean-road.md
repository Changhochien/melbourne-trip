# Great Ocean Road

One of the world's most famous coastal drives — 243km of dramatic coastline, limestone cliffs, surf beaches, and ancient rainforest, west of Melbourne. UNESCO World Heritage site (Royal Melbourne).

## Key Stops (East to West / Tour Direction)

### Lorne
First major town on the Great Ocean Road. Waterfall nearby (Erskine Falls). Good coffee, holiday town. The road gets serious after here.

### Apollo Bay
Gateway to the Otways. Fish and chips on the harbor. Starting point for the Otways rainforest walk (Melbourne person's beach day).

### The Twelve Apostles
The iconic limestone stacks off the coast. Most photographed section. Visitor center, boardwalk. The stacks are constantly eroding — what you see may not be what you see in photos.

### Loch Ard Gorge
5 minutes from the Twelve Apostles. Shipwreck history, more dramatic cliffs and coves. Usually less crowded.

### Port Campbell
Small town at the heart of the limestone coast. Good base for overnight stops. Most of the dramatic coastal formations are accessible from here.

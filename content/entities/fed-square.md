# Federation Square (Fed Square)

Melbourne's primary civic gathering space and cultural precinct, directly opposite [[flinders-street-station]]. Home to major museums, event spaces, and everyday Melburnians watching the Yarra.

## Key Facts
- **Address:** Corner of Swanston St & Flinders St, Melbourne CBD
- **Opened:** 2002
- **Area:** 3.2 hectares

## What's There
- ACMI (Australian Centre for the Moving Image)
- Ian Potter Centre (Indigenous Australian art)
- Koorie Heritage Trust
- Melbourne Visitor Centre
- Birrarung Marr (adjacent park)

## Why It Matters
Despitecontroversial architecture, Fed Square is where Melbourne gathers — for screenings, markets, New Year's Eve, sporting triumphs. The screens above the square run continuous content.

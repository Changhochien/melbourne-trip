# Sovereign Hill

## Overview

Open-air 1850s gold rush museum in Ballarat, Victoria. One of Victoria's most popular tourist attractions — a complete re-creation of a gold rush town with costumed staff, working blacksmith, gold panning, and an underground mine tour.

## What to See

- **Gold Panning** — Included in admission, try your luck in the creek
- **Underground Mine Tour** — Guided descent into 1850s mines, excellent
- **Period Town** — Blacksmith demonstrations, candle shop, costumed staff
- **Wadawurrung Cultural Precinct** — Traditional owners of the Ballarat region
- **Australian Centre for Rare Arts & Forgotten Trades** — Workshops and masterclasses
- **Gold Museum** — Adjacent, included in admission
- **AURA** — Night sound and light show, separate evening entry

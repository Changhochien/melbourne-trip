# Melbourne Stay & Transport Research

## Best Areas to Stay (Cost-Effective + Convenient)

### CBD / Flinders Street area ★★★★★
- Best for: First-time visitors, convenience
- Walking distance to: Fed Square, laneways, QVM, trams
- Mid-range hotels: $150-250/night
- YHA Metro, Space Hotel,鹰巢口袋
- Walkability: Excellent

### Fitzroy ★★★★☆
- Best for: Alternative vibe, foodies, pub culture
- Tram 11/12 to CBD (~15 min)
- Budget: $120-200/night Airbnb
- Walkability: Excellent
- Nightlife nearby

### St Kilda ★★★★☆
- Best for: Beach, relaxed atmosphere
- Tram 96 to CBD (~25 min)
- Budget: $130-200/night
- Walkability: Good

### South Yarra / Prahran ★★★☆☆
- Best for: Shopping, upscale but convenient
- Train to CBD (~10 min)
- Slightly cheaper than CBD

## Airport Transport — Melbourne Airport (MEL) ↔ CBD

| Option | Cost | Time | Notes |
|--------|------|------|-------|
| SkyBus | $22.60/person | ~30 min | To Southern Cross Station. Frequent. Buy online. |
| Taxi/RideShare | ~$50-70 | 30-50 min | Traffic dependent |
| Train (2025 update) | ~$20/person | ~30 min | Still under construction, check status |
| Hotel shuttle | Free-$15 | varies | If hotel provides |

**Recommendation for 2 people:** SkyBus at $45两人 vs taxi $60两人. If sharing Airbnb pickup, taxi/ride might work better.

## Getting Around Melbourne

### Myki Card — Essential
- Buy at 7-Eleven or机场便利店 ($6 deposit)
- Top up $20-30 for 7 days
- Zone 1 covers all inner Melbourne

### Tram — Best for CBD
- Free Tram Zone covers CBD (no touch-on needed)
- Useful routes: 96 (St Kilda), 109 (Box Hill), 12 (Victoria Street)

### Car Rental — Needed for Peninsula Days
- Budget: $50-100/day
- Companies: Thrifty, Budget, Europcar, JUCY
- Book early for July (school holidays = busy)
- International license OK with English translation

## Suggested 7-Night Accommodation Plan
- Nights 1-6 (Jul 1-7): CBD hotel (walking access, tram access)
- Night 7 (Jul 7): Move to Mornington Peninsula (Rye recommended)
- Night 8 (Jul 8): Still Peninsula OR back to CBD same night
- Night 9 (Jul 9): CBD or nearby (early flight = airport area)

# Day 9 Options — Melbourne (Jul 9)

## Option A: Yarra Valley Wine Region
- **Healesville** — Small town, Yarra Valley dairy and wine
- **Healesville Sanctuary** — Australian wildlife (koala, kangaroo, platypus)
- **Yarra Valley wineries** — Cold climate pinot noir, chardonnay
- 1-hour drive from Melbourne CBD
- Note: Different region from Great Ocean Road Day 2 — avoids redundancy

## Option B: Melbourne Inner Exploration (Weather Backup)
- **Queen Victoria Market** — Sunday is the last market day
- **National Gallery of Victoria (NGV)** — Free entry, world-class
- **Melbourne Cricket Ground (MCG)** — Tour or AFL game (winter = AFL season)
- **State Library Victoria** — Stunning historic dome room, free

## Option C: Day Trip — Great Ocean Road Western Approach
- **Mount Buninyong** — Volcanic crater, panoramic views
- **Heathfield** — Famous bakery, start of Great Ocean Road
- Note: If you skipped Ballarat/Sovereign Hill, could add here

## Recommendation
Given Day 2 = Great Ocean Road and Day 3 = Sovereign Hill:
- **Day 9 = Yarra Valley** — completely different experience (wine + wildlife)
- Or **MCG + NGV** combo if weather is bad (cold/wet July)

## Day 10 — Departure (Jul 10)
- Evening flight — full day available
- Morning: Last-minute shopping or return to favorite spot
- Midday: Check out of accommodation
- 3-4 hours before flight: Head to airport
  - SkyBus from Southern Cross Station ($22.60/person, ~30 min)
  - Or taxi/rideshare from hotel (~$55)

# Mornington Peninsula Accommodation Research

> Research updated April 13, 2026. July school holidays = Victorian Term 3 holidays (starts ~Jun 27). Book NOW.

## Areas to Stay
- **Rye** — Central location, beach town, most accommodation options
- **Sorrento** — More upmarket, beachside, ferry to Queenscliff
- **Mount Martha** — Upscale, quiet, beach access
- **Portsea** — Luxury end, at the tip near Point Nepean
- **Flinders** — Small town, wine region, peaceful

## Hot Springs (Mornington Peninsula Hot Springs)
- Located in Mount Martha
- Open-air geothermal pools, hillside bathing
- Book ahead — fills up fast
- Typical session: 3-4 hours
- Combined with accommodation packages available

## Budget/Cost-Effective Options (2026 RATES)
- Airbnb in Rye (most bang for buck, $150-280/night for 2BR apartment)
- Holiday parks in Rye ($120-200 for cabin)
- Motels in Rosebud ($130-200)
- Hotels in Sorrento tend to be pricier ($280-500+)

## Recommended: Stay in Rye
- Most central to hot springs, beaches, and ferry
- Best value accommodation density
- 30-40 min drive to Melbourne CBD
- Can drive back to city after hot springs Day 8, or stay another night

## Getting There
- From Melbourne CBD: M1 south ~1 hour to Rye
- From hot springs: ~15 min drive
- Car essential for peninsula

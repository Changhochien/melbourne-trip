# Sovereign Hill Research

## What It Is
An open-air museum in Ballarat, 115km northwest of Melbourne, recreating an 1850s gold rush town. One of Victoria's most popular tourist attractions. You pan for real gold, ride a horse-drawn carriage, go underground in a mine shaft, and see the town come alive with costumed characters.

## Why It Exists
Ballarat was the centre of the Victorian gold rush — in 1851 it had the world's deepest gold mine and was briefly the largest city in Australia. Sovereign Hill, opened 1970, tells the story of the thousands of migrants who came to seek their fortune and built Melbourne's prosperity in the process.

## Key Activities

### Gold Panning
Real gold panning in the creek — gold is included in the ticket. Most visitors find gold flakes. Kids love it.

### Underground Mine Tour
Descend 180 feet into a reconstructed mine shaft and hear the story of Chinese miners, engine room workers, and the conditions underground. Well-done and atmospheric.

### Horse-Drawn Carriage Rides
The main street (Red Hill) has horse-drawn carriages running regularly.

### The Gold Museum
Adjacent to Sovereign Hill, with a collection of gold nuggets, jewellery, and coins. Also has the world's largest gold bullion bar (71kg).

## Practical Facts
- **Location:** Bradshaw Street, Ballarat, VIC 3350
- **Getting there:** 115km from Melbourne; 1.5 hours by road
- **Best transport:** Guided tour from Melbourne (KKday: ~$75–95/person includes transport)
- **DIY:** Train from Southern Cross to Ballarat (~$20, 1.5 hrs) + 20 min walk or taxi
- **Open:** 10 AM – 5 PM daily
- **Ticket:** Adult ~$40–50; package with Gold Museum ~$55
- **Time needed:** 3–4 hours minimum; full day if you take your time

## July Specifics
July is excellent for Sovereign Hill — most activities are sheltered (Gold Museum, underground mine, coaching inn). The outdoor sections (main street, gold panning) are fine in light rain. Dress warmly. The indoor mine tour is actually better on a cold day — the contrast with the underground warmth is welcome.

## Ballarat
The town of Ballarat surrounds Sovereign Hill. It has some beautiful Victorian architecture and a revitalised CBD. If time permits, a walk past the Town Hall and Lydiard Street (heritage streetscape) is worthwhile.

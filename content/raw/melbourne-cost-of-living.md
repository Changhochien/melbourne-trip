## Daily Budget Estimates (Based on Numbeo)

### Budget Traveller (Lean)
- Food: Cook most meals (~15 AUD/day) + occasional cafe (15 AUD) = ~30 AUD/day × 10 = **300 AUD (~6,738 TWD)**
- Transport: Free Tram Zone + occasional Myki (~5 AUD/day) = ~50 AUD (~1,123 TWD)
- Activities: Free (QVM, laneways, gardens, beaches, street art) + occasional paid (~50 AUD) = **~100 AUD (~2,246 TWD)**
- Daily subtotal: ~45 AUD × 10 = **450 AUD (~10,107 TWD)**

### Mid-Range
- Food: Cafe breakfast (10) + lunch out (20) + cooked dinner (15) = ~45 AUD/day = **450 AUD (~10,107 TWD)**
- Transport: Myki monthly-ish (197 AUD for 10 days = ~65 AUD) = **~65 AUD (~1,460 TWD)**
- Activities: Mix of free + paid tours/events (~150 AUD) = **~150 AUD (~3,369 TWD)**
- Daily subtotal: ~67 AUD × 10 = **~670 AUD (~15,048 TWD)**

### Comfortable
- Food: Restaurant dinners + cafe lunches (60-80 AUD/day) = **~700 AUD (~15,722 TWD)**
- Transport: Myki + taxis (100 AUD) = **~100 AUD (~2,246 TWD)**
- Activities: Tours + experiences (300-500 AUD) = **~400 AUD (~8,984 TWD)**
- Daily subtotal: ~120 AUD × 10 = **~1,200 AUD (~26,952 TWD)**

## Notes for July
- July is off-season; accommodation 20-30% cheaper than summer
- Tours book up for school holidays (late June–early July) — book early
- Long daylight saving (sunset ~5:15 PM) means shorter sightseeing days but less heat

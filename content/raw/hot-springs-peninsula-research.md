## Mornington Peninsula Accommodation — Rye
- **Best value area:** Rye — central, beach town, most accommodation density
- **Price range (July):** $150-280/night for 2-bedroom apartment (good for 2 people)
- **Options:**
  - Airbnb: "Rye apartment" 2BR ~$180-250/night
  - RACV Holiday Park (Rye): Cabin $120-180/night — good family option
  - The Dunes (Rye): Upscale apartments ~$220-300/night
  - Saltbox House (Rye): Cottage ~$180-220/night
- **July school holidays:** Book early — fills up 2-3 weeks ahead
- **Check-in:** Usually 2-3 PM, check-out 10-11 AM

## Car Rental
- **Needed:** Only Days 7-8 (Mornington Peninsula)
- **Companies:** Thrifty, Budget, JUCY, Europcar
- **July rate (economy car):** ~$55-85/day with insurance
- **Pick up:** Melbourne CBD location Day 7 morning
- **Return:** Melbourne CBD or airport Day 8 evening
- **International license:** OK with English translation (Taiwan license + IDP recommended)
- **Insurance:** Full coverage ~$20-30/day extra — recommended for winter driving

## Drive Times
- Melbourne CBD → Rye: ~1 hour 10 min (via M1/M3)
- Rye → Hot Springs (Mount Martha): ~20-25 min
- Hot Springs → Melbourne CBD: ~1 hour 15 min
- Total driving Day 8: ~2.5 hours (minimal — mostly at the hot springs)

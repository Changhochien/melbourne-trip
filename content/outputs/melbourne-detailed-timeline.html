09:00–12:00 Queen Victoria Market Market produce ~$20-40

Key Tips & Reminders

Book Tours NOW
KKday/Klook tours for Days 2, 3, 6 need booking 1-2 weeks ahead for July school holidays. Hot Springs (Day 8) needs booking 1-2 weeks ahead.
Myki Card
Buy at 7-Eleven or airport on arrival. Top up $30-40. Zone 1 covers all inner Melbourne. Free Tram Zone covers CBD.
July Weather
6-14°C, rain 10-12 days. Layers essential. Rain jacket (not umbrella — sideways rain). Warm jacket for penguin parade.
Car Rental — Days 7-8
Reserve NOW. Full coverage insurance recommended. Pick up Day 7 morning, return Day 8 evening at CBD. ~$70-90/day economy.
School Holidays
NSW/Vic school holidays start ~July 1. Expect more families at attractions. Book everything in advance.
Airport — MEL
Melbourne Airport (MEL) — 30 km northwest of CBD. SkyBus is cheapest + most reliable. Uber ~$55-70 from CBD.

# Melbourne Accommodation Areas

Cost-effective + convenient bases for a Melbourne trip. Chosen for walkability to the CBD, good tram connections, and price-to-value ratio.

Slightly too far for a short trip where transit time matters. Better for longer stays or if specifically wanting to shop Richmond's outlet stores.

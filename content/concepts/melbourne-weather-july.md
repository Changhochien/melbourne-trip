# Melbourne Weather — July

July is mid-winter in Melbourne. Cold, short days, unpredictable. Not a bad time to visit — fewer tourists, lower accommodation prices, but cold and occasionally wet.

## Temperature
- **Average high:** 13–14°C (55–57°F)
- **Average low:** 6–7°C (43–45°F)
- **Daylight:** ~10 hours (sunset ~5:15pm)
- **UV:** Low (2-3), not a sun risk

## What to Expect
- Cold mornings and evenings — layers essential
- Rain on ~10-12 days per month
- Grey skies more common than blue
- Occasional cold snaps (0°C or below overnight)
- Sunny days can be pleasant (12-15°C) and feel better than they sound

## What to Pack
- Warm layers (merino base, fleece, warm outer)
- Waterproof jacket — Melbourne rain is sideways
- Scarf and gloves
- Comfortable walking shoes (it will rain)
- Umbrella (but also accept that it won't help with sideways rain)
- Sunglasses (it can be clear and bright even in winter)

## Why July Can Be Good
- Fewer tourists = shorter queues at attractions
- Accommodation significantly cheaper than summer peak
- Great time for museums, cafes, pubs, laneway culture
- Winter sales in shops

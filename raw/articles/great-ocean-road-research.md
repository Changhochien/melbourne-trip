# Great Ocean Road Research

## What It Is
243km coastal road along the Southern Ocean in Victoria, west of Melbourne. UNESCO World Heritage-listed landscape featuring limestone cliff formations, beaches, and historic towns. The most iconic Australian coastal drive.

## Key Stops
- **Lorne** — beach town, coffee stop, 45–60 min
- **Apollo Bay** — lunch stop, 60 min
- **Great Ocean Road Memorial Arch** — photo stop
- **Twelve Apostles** — limestone stacks, 60–90 min
- **Loch Ard Gorge** — shipwreck cove, 40–60 min

## Practical Facts
- **Distance from Melbourne:** ~100km to Apollo Bay, ~240km to the Twelve Apostles
- **Drive time:** 3–4 hours one way without stops; full day with stops
- **Best time:** clear days, but dramatic in rain too
- **July specifics:** Sunset ~5:15–5:30 PM; tours return 9–10 PM; bring layers, rain jacket
- **Guided tour recommended** — self-driving the whole route is long and left-side driving fatigue is real

## Booking
- KKday: ~$89–109 AUD/person (winter rate)
- Klook: ~$79–99 AUD/person (often cheaper)
- Book 1–2 weeks ahead for July school holidays

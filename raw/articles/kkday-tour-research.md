# KKday/Klook Tour Research — Prices & Timings

## Tour 1: Great Ocean Road Day Tour (KKday)
- **KKday URL:** https://www.kkday.com/en/product/129669-great-ocean-road-day-tour-from-melbourne-victoria
- **Klook URL:** https://www.klook.com/en-AU/activity/1826-melbourne-great-ocean-road-sunset-tour/
- **Price (KKday):** ~$89-109 AUD/adult (varies by season, Jul = winter = ~$89-99)
- **Price (Klook):** ~$79-99 AUD/adult (often 10% discount)
- **Duration:** ~14 hours (7:30 AM – 9:30 PM)
- **Departs:** ~7:00-7:30 AM from Melbourne CBD hotels
- **Returns:** ~9:00-9:30 PM
- **Stops:**
  - Lorne (45-60 min) — beach town, coffee stop
  - Apollo Bay (60 min) — lunch break
  - Great Ocean Road memorial arch
  - Twelve Apostles (60-90 min) — main photo stop
  - Loch Ard Gorge (40-60 min) — shipwreck cove
- **Included:** Transport, guide, some meals (lunch usually excluded)
- **What to bring:** Warm layers, rain jacket, walking shoes, camera

## Tour 2: Sovereign Hill Day Trip (KKday)
- **KKday URL:** https://www.kkday.com/en/product/10169 (or similar)
- **Price:** ~$75-95 AUD/adult
- **Duration:** ~10 hours (8:00 AM – 6:00 PM)
- **Departs:** ~8:00 AM
- **Returns:** ~5:30-6:00 PM
- **Stops:**
  - Sovereign Hill (3-4 hours) — gold panning, underground mine tour
  - Gold Museum (30 min) — gold jewellery, history
  - Ballarat CBD (if time) — historic buildings
- **Included:** Transport, guide, entry to Sovereign Hill + Gold Museum
- **Note:** Book online for discount vs walk-up price

## Tour 3: Puffing Billy + Phillip Island (KKday)
- **KKday URL:** (multiple products — search "puffing billy phillip island")
- **Price:** ~$105-145 AUD/adult (combined tour is better value than separate)
- **Duration:** ~14 hours (7:00 AM – 9:30-10:00 PM)
- **Departs:** ~7:00 AM
- **Returns:** ~9:30-10:00 PM
- **Stops:**
  - Puffing Billy Railway, Belgrave (60-90 min) — heritage train ride to Lakeside
  - Koala Conservation Centre, Phillip Island (30-45 min) — koala spotting
  - The Nobbies, Phillip Island (20-30 min) — coastal views, seals
  - Penguin Parade (60-90 min) — sunset at Summerland Beach
- **July Penguin Timing:** Sunset ~5:15 PM — arrive 4:00-4:30 PM for good spots
- **Penguin Viewing Tiers:**
  - General viewing: included
  - Penguin Plus (elevated platform): +$20-25/person — recommended
  - Underground viewing: +$30/person — best view
- **Cold warning:** July evening at penguin beach = 5-8°C, windy, exposed. FULL WARM LAYERS essential.

## General KKday Booking Tips
- Book 1-2 weeks early for July (school holidays = popular)
- Look for "free cancellation" options
- Small group = max 12-20 people vs 45-seat coach
- Compare Klook and KKday for same tour — prices differ by 5-15%
- "Skip the line" or "with lunch" packages sometimes worth the premium

## Day 9 Alternative: Yarra Valley

# Melbourne Cost of Living

Melbourne's cost of living profile as reported by Numbeo (April 2026).

## Key Figures (Numbeo April 2026)

| Category | Approximate Cost |
|---|---|
| Meal, inexpensive restaurant | ~NT$449–NT$561 |
| Meal at mid-range restaurant (2ppl) | ~NT$1,796–NT$2,246 |
| Coffee (regular) | ~NT$89–NT$112 |
| Milk bread loaf | ~NT$67–NT$89 |
| Monthly transport (myki pass) | ~NT$2,919 |
| Monthly utilities (apt, electricity + heating) | ~NT$3,369–NT$4,492 |
| Apartment 1BR in CBD | ~NT$44,920–NT$56,150/month |
| Apartment 1BR in inner suburbs | ~NT$31,444–NT$40,428/month |

## Notes

- Melbourne is comparable to Sydney for cost of living but more affordable than Sydney for accommodation
- Outer suburbs (Coburg, Brunswick, Footscray) offer significantly cheaper rent
- Winter months see some accommodation price drops outside peak tourist season

# Flinders Street Station

Melbourne's grand central railway station. Main entry point for suburban rail services. Heritage-listed Edwardian building with a distinctive Federation Square facade.

## Key Facts
- **Address:** Flinders Street / Swanston Street intersection, Melbourne CBD
- **Opening:** 1910
- **Style:** Edwardian baronial /博览建筑
- **Platforms:** 10 underground, 2 above-ground
- **Myki:** Required to exit (touch on at origin station)

## What It's Like
The iconic dome and yellow facade are synonymous with Melbourne. The under-ground platforms are a labyrinth — allow extra time if changing lines. The main entrance at Flinders and Swanston is across from [[fed-square]].

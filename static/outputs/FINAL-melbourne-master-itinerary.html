⚡ Key Tips

Myki Free Tram Zone
Within CBD: roughly Spring St ↔ Flinders St, Lonsdale St ↔ Flinders St. Most trams in this zone = free, no Myki needed.
July Winter Reality
6–14°C, rain 10-12 days. Pack a proper rain jacket (not umbrella — Melbourne wind makes umbrellas useless). Full warm layers for Phillip Island.
Scoot Carry-on
7kg carry-on included. Check-in bag costs extra. Pack essentials in carry-on in case checked bag is delayed.
eSIM — Order Before Departure
Airalo or Nomad eSIM for Australia ~$25-35 USD. Set up before leaving Taiwan. Active on arrival. Works on most modern phones.
QVM Self-Catering
Buy breakfast/lunch supplies at QVM Day 4 morning. Sourdough, cheese, fruit, spreads. Saves $20-30/day vs eating all meals out.
Book Tours Together
Book all 3 tours on the same day for consistency. Same company means same bus/pickup points. KKday and Klook often have different prices — compare.
Penguin Parade Timing
Arrive 4:00-4:30 PM in July. Sunset ~5:15 PM. Penguins come ashore after dark. Standing 90 min in 5-8°C wind — non-negotiable warm gear.
Driving in Melbourne
Left-hand side driving. Victoria uses km/h. Speed cameras are active. Mobile phone use while driving = heavy fines. Winter road conditions are generally fine.
Mornington Peninsula
You need a car for Days 7-8 only. Don't park at hotels in CBD — daily parking $30-50. Leave car at rental pickup location.
Day 9 Flexibility
Check BOM forecast ~5 days out. Bad weather = swap to Melbourne City Rain Day. Both options are great — no bad choice.
Travel Insurance
Book with flights or soon after. ~$60-80/p for 10 days. Covers medical, trip delay, lost bags — worth it for international travel.
Melbourne Coffee Culture
Ask for "flat white" — Melbourne's signature. Hole-in-the-wall laneway cafes often best. Don't tip (not customary in Australia).

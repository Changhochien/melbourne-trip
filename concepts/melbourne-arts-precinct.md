# Melbourne Arts Precinct (Southbank)

Melbourne's formal arts district, stretching along St Kilda Road from the Yarra to the Shrine of Remembrance, and encompassing Southbank and Fishermans Bend. Home to most of the city's major cultural institutions.

## Major Venues

### National Gallery of Victoria (NGV)
Australia's oldest and largest public art museum. Two sites — NGV International (NGV International, 180 St Kilda Road) and the Ian Potter Centre at Fed Square. Free general admission, major exhibitions ticketed.

### Arts Centre Melbourne
Theatre, ballet, opera, comedy — the performing arts hub. Gold-class stage.

### Australian Centre for the Moving Image (ACMI)
Film, TV, and digital culture museum. Devourer of exhibits and screenings. Fed Square.

### Melbourne Arts Precinct at Night
The precinct transforms at night — lit towers, the Yarra reflections, weekend crowds.

## Geographic Context
Walk from [[flinders-street-station]] south along Swanston Street → St Kilda Road → Arts Precinct. Takes about 15 minutes.

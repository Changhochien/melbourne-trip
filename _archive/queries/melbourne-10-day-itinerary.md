# Melbourne 10-Day Itinerary — Jul 1–10

> Synthesized from girlfriend's plan + accommodation/transport research. Wikilinks point to full wiki pages for each place.

## Day 1 — Jul 1 (Arrival Night)
**Stay: Melbourne CBD**

- Arrive Melbourne Airport (MEL) evening
- **SkyBus** to Southern Cross Station ($22.60/person, ~30 min)
- Check in to CBD hotel (recommended: [[melbourne-accommodation-areas]])
- Light dinner nearby — laneway cafe or hotel restaurant
- Rest — big day tomorrow

**Transport:** SkyBus | **Myki:** Top up on arrival at 7-Eleven ($20-30 loaded)

---

## Day 2 — Jul 2 (Wednesday)
**KKday: Great Ocean Road Day Trip**

Full-day tour via KKday. Expected to cover:
- [[great-ocean-road]] eastern stops (Lorne, Apollo Bay)
- The Twelve Apostles, Loch Ard Gorge
- Return evening

**Practical:**
- KKday tour includes transport and guiding — most cost-effective for solo/couple
- Lunch at Apollo Bay ($20-35)
- Wear layers — coastal wind is cold in July
- Book Great Ocean Road day trip in advance (school holidays = fills up)

**Transport:** KKday bus | **Cost:** ~$80-120/person via KKday

---

## Day 3 — Jul 3 (Thursday)
**KKday: Sovereign Hill Day Trip**

Full-day tour via KKday. Covers:
- [[sovereign-hill]] in Ballarat (gold panning, underground mine, period town)
- Gold Museum
- Likely returns late afternoon

**Practical:**
- Wear comfortable walking shoes — lots of outdoor terrain
- 3-4 hours at Sovereign Hill minimum
- Lunch in Ballarat or at Sovereign Hill cafes

**Transport:** KKday bus | **Cost:** ~$70-100/person via KKday

---

## Day 4 — Jul 4 (Friday)
**Melbourne: Markets + CBD**

- **Morning:** [[queen-victoria-market]] — produce, meat, deli, souvenirs
  - Arrive 8-9am for best selection, cash preferred at some stalls
  - Budget: $20-40 for lunch ingredients to self-cater
- **Midday:** [[melbourne-cbd]] laneways walk
  - Hosier Lane (street art — [[melbourne-laneways]])
  - Block Arcade, Royal Arcade (heritage shopping arcades)
  - Collins Street, Little Collins Street
- **Afternoon:** [[fed-square]] + [[melbourne-arts-precinct]]
  - NGV International (free entry) or ACMI (Australian Centre for the Moving Image)
- **Evening:** Dinner in CBD or laneway restaurant

**Transport:** Myki (Free Tram Zone covers CBD) | **Cost:** Market lunch $20-40 or full restaurant

---

## Day 5 — Jul 5 (Saturday)
**Melbourne: Inner Suburbs + Markets**

- **Morning:** [[fitzroy]] exploration
  - Brunswick Street for coffee (experience [[melbourne-coffee-culture]])
  - Gertrude Street for boutiques
  - Smith Street for lunch (newer restaurants, good value)
  - Op shops if interested (vintage finds)
- **Afternoon:** 
  - South Melbourne Market (if open Sat — closes 4pm, go early)
  - OR tram to [[st-kilda]]
    - St Kilda Pier at sunset (walk along the breakwater)
    - Acland Street — Jewish bakery (Dagmar's or St Kilda Cake House)
    - Luna Park entrance photo (rides optional)
- **Evening:** Dinner in Fitzroy or back to CBD

**Transport:** Myki (tram 11/12 to Fitzroy, tram 96 to St Kilda) | **Cost:** $30-50 for meals

---

## Day 6 — Jul 6 (Sunday)
**KKday: Puffing Billy + Phillip Island**

Full-day tour via KKday. Covers:
- [[puffing-billy-phillip-island]] — heritage railway in Dandenong Ranges
- [[puffing-billy-phillip-island]] — Penguin Parade at sunset

**Practical:**
- Very long day — departs ~7:30am, returns ~9-10pm
- Bring warm layers — evening at Penguin Parade is cold and exposed
- Sunset ~5:15pm in July — Penguin Parade timing aligns
- Book penguin viewing online (Penguin Plus recommended for better views)

**Transport:** KKday bus | **Cost:** ~$100-140/person via KKday

---

## Day 7 — Jul 7 (Monday)
**Move to Mornington Peninsula**

- **Morning:** Check out of Melbourne CBD hotel
- **Drive to [[mornington-peninsula|Rye]]** (~1 hour via M1)
  - **Car rental:** Pick up in Melbourne morning of Day 7
  - Book through Thrifty/Budget/JUCY — ~$60-90/day for 2 days (Day 7 + Day 8)
- **Afternoon:** Explore the peninsula
  - Arthurs Seat (scenic drive, lookouts)
  - Cape Schanck lighthouse walk
  - Wine tasting at Red Hill cellar doors
  - Beach walk at Mount Martha or Sorrento
- **Evening:** Check in to accommodation in Rye
  - Airbnb apartment: $150-250/night
  - Holiday park cabin: $100-150/night

**Stay:** [[mornington-peninsula|Rye]] | **Transport:** Self-drive | **Cost:** Accommodation $120-250 + driving

---

## Day 8 — Jul 8 (Tuesday)
**Mornington Peninsula Hot Springs → Return to Melbourne**

- **Morning:** [[mornington-peninsula|Mornington Peninsula Hot Springs]] at Mount Martha
  - Book online — fills up fast (hot springs open 9am)
  - Plan 3-4 hours
- **Lunch:** Rosebud or Sorrento — seaside fish and chips
- **Afternoon options:**
  - More wine tasting in Red Hill
  - Point Nepean National Park walks
  - OR skip afternoon and drive back to Melbourne mid-afternoon
- **Drive back to Melbourne CBD** (~1 hour)
- **Check in:** Melbourne CBD hotel (night 9 base before departure)

**Stay: Melbourne CBD** | **Transport:** Self-drive | **Cost:** Hot springs ~$40-55/person

---

## Day 9 — Jul 9 (Wednesday)
**Melbourne: Free Day**

Options based on what you've already covered:

| Option | What It Is | Best For |
|--------|------------|----------|
| **A: Yarra Valley** | [[yarra-valley]] wine + wildlife. Healesville Sanctuary, 3-4 wineries | If you want a completely different day from Days 2-3 |
| **B: MCG + NGV** | [[melbourne-arts-precinct]] + MCG tour. Indoor, weather-proof for July | If it rains or you want culture/sport |
| **C: Queen Victoria Market** | Return for Sunday market (last full day before departure) | Shopping for souvenirs, produce to take home |
| **D: Relaxed Final Melbourne Day** | [[st-kilda]] morning, Lygon Street (Italian Carlton), shopping | If you want to return to favorites |

**Recommendation:** Yarra Valley (Day 2 covered coast, Day 9 covers wine/farmland — maximum variety). If weather is bad, MCG + NGV is the weather-proof combo.

**Evening:** Final dinner in Melbourne. Consider [[melbourne-coffee-culture]] one last time — a morning coffee on Day 10 before departure.

---

## Day 10 — Jul 10 (Thursday)
**Departure**

- **Morning:** Last walk through laneways, final coffee, last-minute shopping at Melbourne Central or Emporium
- **Midday:** Check out of hotel
- **Afternoon:** 
  - 3-4 hours before flight: head to airport
  - **SkyBus** from Southern Cross Station ($22.60/person, ~30 min)
  - OR rideshare/taxi from hotel (~$50-60)
- **Fly out** from Melbourne Airport (MEL)

**Transport:** SkyBus or taxi | **Cost:** SkyBus $45两人, Taxi $50-60
